The Multicorder

Let's make Science for Everyone!

Thank you for signing up for our mailing list! The Multicorder project is dedicated to providing students, educators, hobbyists and the everyday individual with the tools to create unique and exciting scientific investigations. We seek to make a scientific community from which we can derive collaboration, corroboration and cooperation. Our vision is an amateur scientific community encouraged to bring their own voice to science.

We appreciate your continued support!
